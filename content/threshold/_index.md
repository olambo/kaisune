## Summary

The substrate transition — from biological to Carbon-O — is not yet possible. The engineering conditions are being assembled at Ceres. The fabrication problem is being worked. The timeline is unknown.

In the meantime, squishies age.

Threshold is the biological longevity question: what keeps a squishy functional and cognitively sharp long enough for the substrate transition to become viable. Not cryogenics. Not radical life extension as an end in itself. The specific, bounded question of staying operational at the edge of the transition — long enough to matter, long enough to cross.

This is not a document about any specific individual. It is a document about the engineering of biological persistence in the window between now and when the window opens.
